CENTERSTAGE Events

[12/10/2023] - Qualifier #1, Half Hollow Hills High School West

This was our first teams first ever event. At this qualifier we placed #2 in the qualification rounds and went on to be the 2nd place alliance captain alongside our alliance partner Team 4017, the robopandas.

  • Finalist Alliance Captain
  • Connect Award
  • Control Award 2nd Place
  • Design Award 3rd Place

[1/14/2024] - Qualifier #3, Sewanhaka High School

At this event we once again made it to the finals with our new alliance partner Team 16938, the Mages. At this event we also won the most coveted award, the Inspire Award.

  • Inspire Award
  • Finalist Alliance Captain
  • Design Award 2nd Place
  • Motivate Award 2nd Place
  • Think Award 3rd Place

[2/4/2024] - Qualifier #5, Locust Valley High School

At this event we once again made it to the finals and ending up winning with our new alliance partners Team 5477, Innovo and Team 17384, the Rambam Rambots. This is our teams first win.

  • Winning Alliance Captain
  • Think Award
  • Design Award 2nd Place

[3/3/2024] - Long Island Regionals, Mineola High School

At regionals we had a few mistakes during the qualification matches and we ended up placing 12th. However the 1st ranked team, Team 22942 Iron Phoenix picked us to join their alliance. We went on to get to the finals and lose 1-2. We put up a great fight and it was an amazing first time at a regional championship!

  • Finalist Alliance - 1st Team Seleceted
  • Connect Award
  • Inspire Award 3rd Place
  • Innovate Award 3rd Place
