Friar Industries
FTC Team 23707
Live, Laugh, Love Robotics

Friar Industries is a group of passionate robotics enthusiasts who love to build and program robots. Our program is open to all students who have a keen interest in robotics and want to learn more about it. We meet numerous times week to discuss new ideas, work on projects and participate in competitions. Join us today and become a part of the exciting world of robotics!

Virtual Innovation Center

Spreading Robotics
Spreading FIRST
Educating ROBLOX

At Friar Industries, we're passionate about spreading the values of FIRST Robotics and inspiring the next generation of engineers, innovators, and problem solvers. Through our unique Roblox game, we've created a virtual replica of our robotics lab where players can experience and engage with the real-world challenges of the FIRST Tech Challenge. By blending creativity, teamwork, and technical skills, we aim to bring the excitement of robotics to a wider audience, fostering a love for STEM and the spirit of FIRST in an interactive and fun way. Join us in exploring, learning, and growing with robotics—one game at a time!
